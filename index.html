Instructions
×
  • Click the start button to start the game.
  • Click the first card to turn it over.
  • Click on a second card to try and match the two cards together.
  • When the cards match, they will have the same image and stay turned over.
  • One card will have a word in English and another card will have a word in Portuguese.
  • Use these correctly matched cards to learn words in portuguese.
  • When the game has finished you will be able to make a full sentence in portuguese!
